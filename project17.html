Puzzle Cube - CAD Design Project

2021

This project involved designing a 3×3 mechanical puzzle cube consisting of five interlocking components using Autodesk Inventor. The design required precise geometric constraints and careful consideration of part fit to ensure the cube could be assembled smoothly and function as intended.
Through this project, I developed proficiency in creating detailed CAD models and producing professional IDW (Inventor Drawing) files. This included generating fully dimensioned drawings, exploded assembly views, and parts lists, while following standard engineering drawing conventions and documentation practices.
The project also highlighted the importance of accurate dimensioning and organized CAD workflows. I improved my attention to detail by reviewing dimensions for consistency and learned to manage file structure and assembly order more effectively to support a clear and logical design process.
