Glider Project — Aerodynamic Design & Simulation

2024

This project involved designing and simulating a glider using AERY software while meeting strict performance and design constraints. Through repeated simulations, I learned how individual components contribute to aerodynamic performance and how simulation tools are used to validate designs prior to fabrication.

The most significant challenge was achieving a configuration that met the required AERY performance number. Small design changes frequently resulted in performance violations, requiring extensive iteration. This process strengthened my understanding of constraint-driven design and optimization.

Simulation results revealed that wing geometry had the greatest impact on overall performance. Earlier identification of this sensitivity would have allowed more targeted design iterations and reduced development time prior to final construction.
