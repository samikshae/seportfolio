Iso-Shapes - CAD Design Project

2021

This project focused on recreating a series of isometric shapes from reference images using Autodesk Inventor. Each shape was modeled from scratch, requiring accurate interpretation of geometry and careful use of extrusions and cuts to match the original designs.

Through this work, I developed efficiency in solid modeling by strategically adding and removing material during the extrusion process. The project strengthened my ability to visualize 3D forms from 2D references and translate them into precise CAD models and corresponding IDW drawings.

The project also reinforced the importance of proper dimensioning practices. I identified and corrected issues related to over-dimensioning and chain dimensioning, improving the clarity and accuracy of technical drawings and reinforcing standard engineering documentation conventions.
