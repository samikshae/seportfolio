Fuel Cell Car - Renewable Energy Project

2023

This project involved designing and building a functional fuel-cell-powered car using a hydrogen fuel cell as the primary energy source. The objective was to convert electrical energy produced by the fuel cell into mechanical motion through a motor-driven system.

The car was constructed using a breadboard-based circuit that connected the fuel cell to a DC motor through conductive wiring. This configuration allowed current to flow through the motor, enabling controlled motion of the vehicle. Testing and troubleshooting focused on ensuring proper circuit connections and stable voltage delivery.

Through this project, I gained hands-on experience with breadboarding, circuit construction, and the use of a multimeter to measure voltage in an open circuit. The project reinforced the importance of accurate data collection timing and careful measurement practices when working with electrical systems.
