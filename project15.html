Automata - CAD Design Project

2022

This project involved designing a fully functioning mechanical automaton using Autodesk Inventor. The system required multiple interacting components to be assembled and constrained correctly in order to produce controlled and repeatable motion.

Through this project, I developed a deeper understanding of advanced assembly techniques in Inventor, including the use of geometric and motion constraints to accurately connect and drive components. I gained experience building mechanically functional assemblies rather than static parts, reinforcing principles of kinematics and mechanical interaction.

The project also highlighted the importance of creativity in mechanical design. I identified the need to move beyond convenience-driven solutions and challenge my design approach in order to create more innovative and expressive mechanical components.
